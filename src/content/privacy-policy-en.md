## 3. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the application:

*   **Personal Data:** Email address.
*   **Profile Information:** Interests, gender, age range, and profile picture (selfie).
*   **Usage Data:** Information about how you use the app, including matching history.

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

*   Create and manage your account.
*   Facilitate the matching process with other users based on shared interests.
*   Send you emails regarding your account or matches (Magic Links).
*   Improve our application and user experience.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

*   **Service Providers:** Third-party vendors who perform services for us (e.g., database hosting, email delivery).
*   **Other Users:** Your profile information (interests, age range, gender, avatar) is visible to users you are matched with.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy, or until you request its deletion.

*   Access your personal data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us.
